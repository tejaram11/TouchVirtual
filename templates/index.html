Touch Virtual

AS COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY CONTINUES TO DEVOLOP,PEOPLE HAVE SMALLER ELECTRONIC DEVICES. INCREASINGLY WE ARE RECOGNIZING THE IMPORTANCE OF HUMAN COMPUTING INTERACTIONS(HCI), AND INPARTICULAR VISION-BASED GESTURE AND OBJECT RECOGNITION. IN OUR PROJECT , WE PROPOSE A NOVEL APPROACH THAT USES A VIEDO DEVICE TO CONTROL THE MOUSE SYSYTEM(MOUSE TASKS). WE EMPLOY SEVERAL IMAGES PROCESSING ALGORITHM TO IMPLEMENT THIS.

Actions:
1. move index finger to move MOUSE
2. join index finger and thumb for right click
3. join index finger and middle finger for left click
